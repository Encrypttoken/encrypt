Staking on Encrypt Chain

A token holder can delegate their tokens to one of the validators to receive staking rewards. Rewards are earned proportionally based on the amount of delegated tokens. Since only bonded validators receive staking rewards and fee distributions, only those who delegate to bonded validators are eligible to earn the corresponding rewards. The minimum staking amount per transaction is 5,000,000 ECT. Detailed reward rates and the token minting/burning process are discussed in the staking section.

Encrypt Token

The Encrypt token (ECT) is the native currency of the Encrypt Chain, serving as the fundamental medium of exchange and utility within our ecosystem. ECT is integral to accessing various features and services on the Encrypt Chain, including staking, transaction fees, and governance voting. By holding and utilizing ECT, users can actively participate in the network, influence its direction, and benefit from its growth and success.

The Encrypt tokenomics model is designed to ensure the sustainability and long-term value appreciation of ECT, with mechanisms in place to control supply and incentivize holding and usage. As the Encrypt Chain ecosystem expands and evolves, the demand for ECT is expected to grow, further driving its value and utility.
